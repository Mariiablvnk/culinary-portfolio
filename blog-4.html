About me

I’m Mariia, a home cook with a passion for exploring diverse cuisines and cultures

My interest in food deepened during my university years, where I worked as a waitress in Asian and meat restaurants. That experience inspired me to step into the kitchen and explore cooking on a deeper level.

My culinary journey has been shaped by living in Spain, Serbia, and Italy, where I immersed myself in Mediterranean and Balkan food cultures, honing my skills through home cooking.

Recently, I gained hands-on experience as an ayuda de cocina in a Mexican restaurant in southern Spain, refining my techniques and learning the fast-paced dynamics of a professional kitchen.

In the kitchen, I am highly focused, hardworking, and respectful of culinary techniques and traditions. Though I’m still building my experience, I bring dedication and a strong eagerness to learn. I’m open to any opportunities, whether as a kitchen helper or a home cook, and I’m excited to grow in the world of culinary arts.
